About

I've led many lives including a musician, journalist, broadcaster and most recently as a book author. Through it all my approach has been to dive deep into things I know little about and come out making things and sharing what I know.

Most recently I went through the web design track at the Startup Institute, which was eight exhilirating weeks of learning, doing and diving into the Chicago startup world.

I'm looking to join a company where I can use the skills I have and expand on some new ones. I have some ideas of my own, but I'm interested in yours. Let's talk and make cool things happen.

Around the Web

Hiroshima Nagasaki: An Illustrated History, Anthology, and Guide

Wrote the content, assembled the photos and extracts used in it, secured copyrights. Over the course of the 10-month project I traveled to Japan and witnessed the Peace Ceremonies in both cities and met several people involved with preserving the memory of these events. I worked with the team at Odyssey to make sure this book looked good and was accurate, and managed to get it out on time despite the very fast turnaround and other pressures facing the company. The book ended up being released one week before the 70th anniversary of the atomic bombings.

  • Client: Odyssey Publications
  • Date: July 30, 2015
